About us

A dog, a cat and a parrot ... How much joy and warmth these cheerful friends bring to our house.

Under the terms of the campaign, our clinic offers a free "Daily Inpatient" service, which will save you time. You have the opportunity to go with us.

When deciding whether to have a pet, or already having one,we must not forget that an animal is a living organism that requires not only our love, but also care for its health and appearance. The owner's responsibility is to strictly follow the rules for caring for animals, to consult a doctor in a timely manner for advice and help in resolving any issue. After all, we are responsible for those we have tamed!

The veterinary clinic provides a wide range of services, ranging from simple preventive measures to complex surgical procedures. We carry out X-ray and ultrasound examinations, as well as general clinical and biochemical blood tests. A general urine analysis with sediment microscopy is performed.


Only here. You can test your cat's blood for diseases such as panleukopenia, calcivirosis, herpesvirus infection, coronavirus, toxoplasmosis, and chlamydia. And also dogs for diseases such as infectious hepatitis, parvovirus enteritis, carnivore plague. The full range of services can be found here.

Do you want to trim your pet?

There is a groomer's office in the hospital, after visiting which, your pet will be especially gorgeous and attractive.
